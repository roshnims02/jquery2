One of the beautiful states in our country is Kerala. Kerala is situated in the south western region of India. A state filled with natural beauty, Kerala is also known as “Gods Own Country”. Kerala is an extraordinary state that has many attractive points inside it. The states traditional dances are Kathakali and Mohini Aatam. Indian elephant is considered the official animal of the state. People of Kerala speak Malayalam; and it is the official language of the state.
